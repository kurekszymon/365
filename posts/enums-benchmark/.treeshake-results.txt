note: surprise outcome. esbuild understands `enum` semantics well enough to
DCE the entire IIFE when only one member is referenced — `Direction.Up` is
inlined as `"UP"`, the rest vanishes. `as const` objects, by contrast, are
plain expressions the bundler can't prove side-effect-free, so the whole
literal ships. string literal union is type-only, so the import vanishes.
